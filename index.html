Skellig more_vert

28 July 2016

Skellig close

Skellig is a magic realism young adult novel about a family who moves into a new home, and the children discover what appears to be an angel living in the shed. The angel’s a mess: dirty and slowly starving, living on insects and mice, and spitting up what appears to be owl pellets.

No, this is not your traditional, squeaky clean, radiant angel. He’s much better.
